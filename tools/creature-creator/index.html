Creature Creator

Add limb images, position them, and connect them with joints. Right-click the canvas or press + Joint to add a joint. Hide a limb to lock it out of the way while you work. Select a limb or joint and nudge it with the arrow keys (hold Shift for larger steps). Export joint data writes each joint's offset from its connected limb centers to JSON. Export full creature saves a zip of the images and layout that import full creature can reload.
