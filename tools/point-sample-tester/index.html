Point Sample Tester

Add images and the canvas redraws them as a grid of point-sampled cells. Drag an image to move it, drag its corner handle to scale proportionally, and drag the top handle to rotate (it snaps to 0, 90, 180, 270). Adjust the cell size, cell shape, and color quantization on the left. Scroll to zoom, middle-click drag to pan, and reset zoom for 1:1.
